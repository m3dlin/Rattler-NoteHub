Rattler NoteHub

Welcome to Rattler NoteHub! Use the tabs below to view the platform guidelines and usage tips.

Upload Your Own Notes

Only upload notes that you have created yourself. Uploading graded assignments, test material, or copyrighted content is not allowed.

Upload in PDF Format

Ensure your notes are in PDF format as it's the only supported file type.

Notes Visibility Settings

Choose whether your notes are public or private. You can update their visibility status at any time.

Maintain Account Security

Do not share your login credentials. You are responsible for all content uploaded through your account.

Report Suspicious Activity

Report any unusual activity or unauthorized content to the website developer immediately.

Use Descriptive Titles

Give your notes meaningful titles so they are easier to find through search.

Contact Support

If you need help, reach out to the developer's email: emedlin@mail.stmarytx.edu.

Navigation

Use the top navigation bar to move between Home, Guidelines, and your Inbox. Logout using the top right button.

Adding Courses

Next to the 'Courses' section on the Home menu, select the '+' icon to begin adding courses to your account.

Uploading Notes

Go to the 'My Notes' section on the Home menu and select the '+' icon to upload your PDF files. Fill in the correct note information, and set its visibility as public or private.

Viewing Notes

Visit a course page to browse or search for notes. Use filters to sort by course or popularity. You can only view notes from the courses you have added.

Discussion Boards

Each course will have a discussion board section. Here, you can view or create discussion posts about anything related to the course.

Commenting

Click on a note to open its detail view. You can comment to ask questions or give any feedback to the original poster.

Checking Your Inbox

At the top right is your Inbox. This contains messages whenever someone interacts with your discussion posts or notes. You can view the original content where that message came from or you can delete the inbox message
