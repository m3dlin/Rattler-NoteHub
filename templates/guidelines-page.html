Rattler NoteHub Guidelines

Welcome to Rattler NoteHub!
Below are some guidelines to ensure a smooth and secure experience for all users:

1. Upload Your Own Notes

Only upload notes that you have created yourself. Do not upload notes that you do not have permission to share. This means that uploading test material or other graded assignments is prohibited.

2. Upload PDF Format

Upload your notes in PDF format. Other formats are not supported.

3. Notes Can Be Private Or Public

Your uploaded notes can either be private or public. You can set the status of the note at any time.

4. Maintain Account Security

Keep your account credentials secure and do not share them with anyone. You are responsible for the content uploaded from your account.

5. Report Suspicious Activity

If you notice any suspicious activity or unauthorized access to your account, report it immediately to the website administrators.

6. Descriptive Title

Put a good description on notes to search up easier.

7. Contact Us for Assistance

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team. We're here to help!


Thank you for using Rattler NoteHub!
