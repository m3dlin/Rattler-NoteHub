Rattler NoteHub Guidelines

Welcome to Rattler NoteHub! Please follow these guidelines to help us maintain a respectful and effective platform for all users:

Upload Your Own Notes

Only upload notes that you have created yourself. Uploading graded assignments, test material, or copyrighted content is not allowed.

Upload in PDF Format

Ensure your notes are in PDF format as it's the only supported file type.

Notes Visibility Settings

Choose whether your notes are public or private. You can update their visibility status at any time.

Maintain Account Security

Do not share your login credentials. You are responsible for all content uploaded through your account.

Report Suspicious Activity

Report any unusual activity or unauthorized content to the website developer immediately.

Use Descriptive Titles

Give your notes meaningful titles so they are easier to find through search.

Contact Support

If you need help, reach out to the developer's email: emedlin@mail.stmarytx.edu
